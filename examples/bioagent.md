- **[Empowering biomedical discovery with AI agents](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cell.2024.09.022)**  
  *Journal: Cell*  
  *Published: 31 October 2024*  
  Summary: This paper discusses the concept of AI scientists as systems that integrate AI models and biomedical tools with experimental platforms to enhance biomedical research. It outlines how these AI agents can complement human expertise by analyzing large datasets, navigating hypothesis spaces, and performing repetitive tasks, thus proposing applications in areas like virtual cell simulation and therapy development.

- **[The application of large language models in medicine: A scoping review](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.isci.2024.109713)**  
  *Journal: iScience*  
  *Published: 17 May 2024*  
  Summary: This study systematically reviewed the application of large language models (LLMs) in medicine, analyzing 550 selected studies. LLMs enhanced diagnostics, medical writing, education, and project management. Despite their utility, challenges like contextual limitations and over-reliance remain.

- **[A framework for human evaluation of large language models in healthcare derived from literature review](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41746-024-01258-7)**  
  *Journal: npj Digital Medicine*  
  *Published: 28 September 2024*  
  Summary: This study proposes the QUEST framework for evaluating LLMs in healthcare. It reviews existing methodologies and outlines a systematic approach focusing on various dimensions to ensure efficacy in clinical settings.

- **[Artificial Intelligence in Biological Sciences](https://doi.org/10.3390/life12091430)**  
  *Journal: Life (Basel)*  
  *Published: 14 September 2022*  
  Summary: The article discusses AI's integration in biological sciences, focusing on applications in drug discovery and personalized medicine, while highlighting its role as a solution provider in various challenges faced in these fields.

- **[Assessing the research landscape and clinical utility of large language models: a scoping review](https://bmcmedinformdecismak.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s12911-024-02459-6)**  
  *Journal: BMC Medical Informatics and Decision Making*  
  *Published: 12 March 2024*  
  Summary: This review examines the accuracy and efficacy of LLMs in medical applications, discussing ethical and implementation issues and proposing frameworks for assessing their clinical utility. 

- **[How natural language processing derived techniques are used on biological data: a systematic review](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s13721-024-00458-1)**  
  *Journal: Network Modeling Analysis in Health Informatics and Bioinformatics*  
  *Published: 06 May 2024*  
  Summary: This review evaluates 82 studies on using NLP techniques in biological data analysis, revealing advancements in bioinformatics and emphasizing the importance of methodologies like word2vec and transformers.

- **[Large language models and their applications in bioinformatics](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.csbj.2024.09.031)**  
  *Journal: Computational and Structural Biotechnology Journal*  
  *Published: December 2024*  
  Summary: The review highlights the potential of LLMs in bioinformatics, particularly in genomics, proteomics, and drug discovery, underlining their role in advancing healthcare and accelerating discoveries.

- **[Machine Learning in Healthcare](https://doi.org/10.2174/1389202922666210705124359)**  
  *Journal: Current Genomics*  
  *Published: 16 December 2021*  
  Summary: This overview discusses ML-based approaches in various healthcare fields, touching on advances in predicting health emergencies and challenges for future applications.

- **[The next generation of evidence-based medicine](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-022-02160-z)**  
  *Journal: Nature Medicine*  
  *Published: 16 January 2023*  
  Summary: The article explores how advancements in wearable technologies and data science are reshaping evidence-based medicine, advocating for a more patient-centered approach in clinical trials.
