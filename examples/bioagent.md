# The Applications of LLM-Based Agents in Biology and Medicine

Large Language Models (LLMs) are transforming the fields of biology and medicine by enhancing research methodologies, patient care, and clinical processes. This collection of papers explores various applications of LLM-based agents, showcasing their potential and addressing challenges in these domains.

### Key Papers
- **[Empowering biomedical discovery with AI agents](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0092867424010705)**  
  *Cell, 31 October 2024*  
  Envisions "AI scientists" that enhance biomedical research through collaborative agents that integrate AI models and biomedical tools.

- **[Generative AI agents are transforming biology research: high resolution functional genome annotation for multiscale understanding of life](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ebiom.2024.105446)**  
  *eBioMedicine, November 8, 2024*  
  Discusses the impact of generative AI agents in genome annotation, drug development, and biological systems design.

- **[Empowering Biomedical Discovery with AI Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.02831v1)**  
  *arXiv, 03 April 2024*  
  Reviews the integration of AI tools in biomedical research and their capacities in hypothesis generation and data processing.

- **[Large language models empowered agent-based modeling and simulation: a survey and perspectives](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41599-024-03611-3)**  
  *Humanities and Social Sciences Communications, 27 September 2024*  
  Surveys the integration of LLMs in agent-based simulations across various domains.

- **[LLMs and generative agent-based models for complex systems research](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.plrev.2024.10.013)**  
  *Physics of Life Reviews, December 2024*  
  Reviews the impact of LLMs on complex systems, focusing on social dynamics and epidemic modeling.

- **[The application of large language models in medicine: A scoping review](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.isci.2024.109713)**  
  *iScience, 17 May 2024*  
  A systematic review of LLM applications in medical diagnostics, writing, and project management.

- **[The future landscape of large language models in medicine](https://www.nature.com/articles/s43856-023-00370-1)**  
  *Communications Medicine, 10 October 2023*  
  Reviews potential applications and challenges of LLMs in clinical practice and medical education.

- **[Embracing Large Language Models for Medical Applications: Opportunities and Challenges](https://doi.org/10.7759/cureus.39305)**  
  *Cureus, 21 May 2023*  
  Discusses advancements enabled by LLMs in diagnostic accuracy and clinical decision-making.

- **[Practical Applications of Large Language Models for Health Care Professionals and Scientists](https://medinform.jmir.org/2024/1/e58478)**  
  *JMIR Medical Informatics, 5 September 2024*  
  Highlights effective applications of LLMs for healthcare professionals, addressing integration challenges.

- **[Med-PaLM](https://sites.research.google/med-palm/)**  
  *Nature, July 2023*  
  Introduces a specialized LLM from Google Research for medical question answering.

- **[Large Language Models in Healthcare and Medical Domain: A Review](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.06775v2)**  
  *arXiv, 8 July 2024*  
  Surveys existing LLM applications in healthcare, focusing on clinical text understanding and ethics.

- **[Language models for biological research: a primer](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41592-024-02354-y)**  
  *Nature Methods, 9 August 2024*  
  A guide for biologists on effectively utilizing language models in their research.

- **[Evaluating large language models as agents in the clinic](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41746-024-01083-y)**  
  *npj Digital Medicine, 3 April 2024*  
  Proposes a framework to evaluate the effectiveness of LLM agents in clinical settings.

- **[CT-Agent: Clinical Trial Multi-Agent with Large Language Model-based Reasoning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.14777v1)**  
  *arXiv, 23 April 2024*  
  Introduces a framework for enhancing clinical trial processes using LLMs.

- **[Large language models in biomedicine and health: current research landscape and future directions](https://doi.org/10.1093/jamia/ocae202)**  
  *Journal of the American Medical Informatics Association, 22 August 2024*  
  Overview of the research landscape and future of LLMs in biomedicine.

- **[Learning to match patients to clinical trials using large language models](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jbi.2024.104734)**  
  *Journal of Biomedical Informatics, November 2024*  
  Explores the use of LLMs for improving patient recruitment in clinical trials through semantic analysis.

These studies collectively highlight the transformative potential of LLMs in biology and medicine, while also acknowledging the challenges that come with their integration into these critical fields.
